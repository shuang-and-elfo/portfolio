Smart Campaigns

Google Ads is incredibly powerful‚Äîbut that power comes with complexity. For small business owners juggling a hundred responsibilities, learning a complex ad platform isn't realistic.

The Challenge

How might we help small businesses successfully advertise on Google without requiring them to become advertising experts?

The Approach

Smart Campaigns strips away the complexity of Google Ads while preserving its effectiveness. Instead of asking users to set bids, choose keywords, and optimize placements, we focused on what they actually know: their business goals.

  • Goal-focused setup: Start with "What do you want customers to do?" not "Set your CPC bid"
  • AI-powered optimization: Let Google's systems handle the technical details
  • Plain language: Replace jargon with terms business owners understand
  • Quick setup: Get a campaign running in under 15 minutes

Google Shopping Homepage

The Google Shopping homepage was underutilized‚Äîusers went directly to search, missing opportunities for discovery. I led the redesign to make it a destination for browsing and finding deals.

Key Features

Personalized Discovery

Created a feed of products based on user interests, search history, and shopping patterns‚Äîbringing the serendipity of in-store browsing to online shopping.

Price Tracking

Designed a system that lets users track prices on products they're interested in, receiving notifications when prices drop. This drove repeat visits and user loyalty.

Deal Surfacing

Built prominent placements for sales, deals, and limited-time offers‚Äîgiving users a reason to check back regularly.

Key Insight

Shopping isn't just about finding specific items‚Äîit's also about discovery. By making browsing enjoyable, we could capture a different user intent entirely.
