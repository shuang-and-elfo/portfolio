A bit about me

Hey there! My name is Shuang Li, a product designer with 10+ years of experience building products used by millions of people. I love turning ambiguous, high-stakes problems into clear, intuitive experiences that feel good to use. I currently lead design for Snapchat+ and ad formats at Snap, and before that I spent several years at Google designing across Google Ads, Shopping, and Chrome.

When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me bouldering. I love climbing outdoors at places like Bishop and Red Rocks; the problem-solving, the fresh air, and the community are my favorite way to reset.

A lifetime ago, I ran a small YouTube channel teaching Mandarin-speaking climbers. It was niche but fun, and every now and then someone still recognizes me at a climbing gym.

Outside of work and climbing, I live in Los Angeles with my two young kids and our corgi, Elfo, who collectively keep life delightfully chaotic. I'm also an Airbnb Superhost, which lets me create thoughtful, memorable experiences for people in the real world.
