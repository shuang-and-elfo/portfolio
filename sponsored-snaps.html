Overview

As a key design leader for Snap's advertising products, I drive the strategy and design for ad formats that power our $5B ad business. This includes Sponsored Snaps, Dynamic Product Ads (DPA), and other formats across Snapchat's surfaces.

My work focuses on finding the balance between business growth and user trust‚Äîensuring ads deliver value to advertisers while respecting the unique, intimate nature of Snapchat's experience.

The Challenge

How do we scale ad revenue across Snapchat's most personal surfaces without compromising the trust and engagement that makes our platform valuable in the first place?

The Strategic Tension

Every ad format decision sits at the intersection of competing needs:

  • Business: Snap needs new ad inventory to grow revenue and compete
  • Users: Snapchat's intimate surfaces demand respect‚Äîany intrusion feels personal
  • Advertisers: Brands want high visibility, engagement, and performance

My role is to navigate these tensions strategically‚Äîfinding solutions that grow the business while protecting what makes Snapchat special.

Design Principles

Before diving into design, I established principles that would guide every decision:

Transparent, not deceptive

Ads must be clearly distinguishable from friend content at a glance.

Respectful of attention

No autoplay, no forced views‚Äîusers always have control.

Easy to dismiss

Getting rid of an ad should be as easy as dismissing a notification.

Frequency with care

Strict limits on how often ads appear to prevent fatigue.
